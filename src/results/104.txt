Eastbourne, EnglandEastbourne, EnglandProgram Highlights
Students will study Curriculum and Instruction in the Master's Program at Eastbourne, England. Teaching methods will range from whole group for Core classes, smaller groups for electives, and even smaller groups for the tutor directed independent study. Students will also be able to visit other schools in the area as well as participate in a number of field trips to sites such as London, Oxford, Stratford-upon-Avon, West and East Sussex, Rye and Bodiam Castle, Warwick Castle, Paris and other places of interest. University/College Highlights
